- **Mode of Appropriation** — systematic identification, collection, and synthesis of knowledge
- **Mode of Inquiry** — temporal and dialectical analysis through the 42 Laws of Development
- **Mode of Presentation** — structured output from simple to complex (cell → category → group → series → system)
